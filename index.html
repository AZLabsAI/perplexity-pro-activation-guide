Steps

Activation steps

Follow the sequence in order. Do not continue to payment until the discount is applied and the total shown at checkout is €0.

STEP 01

Go to the Perplexity website

Perplexity Pro procedure

Start at the main Perplexity website before doing anything else.

  • Open the site in your browser.
  • Use the same browser session for the full activation flow.
STEP 02

Sign in to your account

Perplexity Pro procedure

Log in before you try to change the pricing plan.

  • Only new accounts with no previous Pro subscriptions are accepted.
  • Do not continue with an account that has already had Perplexity Pro before.
STEP 03

Click Update your pricing plan

Perplexity Pro procedure

Once you are signed in, open the pricing flow from your account.

  • Find the option labeled "Update your pricing plan".
  • This takes you to the checkout page where the billing settings can be changed.
STEP 04

Switch to Annual Billing

Perplexity Pro procedure

The checkout must be on annual billing before you apply the code.

  • Do not leave the billing cycle on monthly.
  • Confirm the annual option is selected before moving to the next field.
STEP 05

Select the currency of your bank country

Perplexity Pro procedure

Use the currency that matches your bank country at checkout.

  • Choose the correct currency from the available list.
  • Make sure the billing information matches the selected country and currency.
STEP 06

Enter your Promo Code

Perplexity Pro procedure

Apply your promo code in the dedicated field on the checkout form.

  • Enter the code exactly as provided to you.
  • Wait for the checkout summary to refresh after applying it.
STEP 07

Confirm the total becomes €0

Perplexity Pro procedure

Before paying, verify that the discount has fully reduced the total.

  • The total should display €0 after the code is accepted.
  • If the price does not change, stop and troubleshoot before continuing.
STEP 08

Enter your card details and billing address

Perplexity Pro procedure

Complete the payment form only after the discounted total is correct.

  • Use a valid debit card, preferably Visa or Mastercard.
  • Enter your existing billing address exactly as your bank expects it.
STEP 09

Finish the €0 payment and cancel auto-renewal if needed

Perplexity Pro procedure

After the successful €0 checkout, you can disable renewal from settings.

  • Complete the payment flow and wait for confirmation.
  • If you do not want renewal later, go to your settings and click "Cancel Subscription".

Checks

Troubleshooting

If the promo code does not work or checkout fails, verify these conditions before retrying the activation.

If the promo code is invalid

Make sure you are using a new account, enter a valid debit card, and if the problem continues change your IP to another European country before trying the activation again.

What if the promo code says it is invalid?

Check that the account is new, make sure a valid debit card is entered, and if it still fails change your IP to another European country before trying again.

Can I use an account that already had Perplexity Pro before?

No. The source instructions say only new accounts with no previous Pro subscriptions are accepted.

When should I cancel the subscription?

Only after the €0 payment succeeds. Then you can open settings and use the cancel subscription option if you do not want auto-renewal.
